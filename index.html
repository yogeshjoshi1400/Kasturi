About Kasturi Ghar

A home for
local produce &
local business

Kasturi Ghar — meaning "house of musk" — is a Detroit-based initiative focused on promoting the freshest local produce alongside the vibrant small businesses that make our city unique.

We believe that buying local isn't just a transaction — it's an act of community. Every purchase supports a neighbor, a family farm, or an independent maker working hard to bring quality goods to our tables.

From seasonal Michigan produce to handcrafted goods, we bring you the best of Detroit's growing ecosystem.

🌱

Farm Fresh

Direct relationships with Michigan farms ensure the freshest seasonal produce year-round.

🏘️

Community Rooted

Every dollar spent here stays local, supporting Detroit families and independent businesses.

♻️

Sustainably Minded

We prioritize seasonal, low-impact sourcing that respects both people and the planet.

Our Community

How we come together

Kasturi Ghar is more than a store — it's a gathering point for Detroit's South Asian community and local food lovers alike.

🌾

Support Local Farmers

We partner directly with Michigan family farms to ensure producers receive fair prices while you receive the freshest possible products, straight from the source.

🏪

Amplify Small Business

From artisan food makers to cultural goods sellers, we actively promote and feature independent Detroit businesses that deserve a wider audience.

🤝

Cultural Connection

We celebrate the diversity of Detroit by honoring the culinary traditions and community ties that make this city extraordinary.

🎓

Youth Programs

Through initiatives like Nepal Lego, we invest in the next generation — combining education, creativity, and cultural pride for Detroit's youth.

📣

Events & Pop-Ups

Regular community events, farmers markets, and cultural celebrations throughout the year in Detroit and surrounding areas.

💛

Give Back

A portion of every sale supports community initiatives, food access programs, and local causes that strengthen Detroit neighborhoods.
